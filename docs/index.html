Privacy Policy — Pipe Avoider

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Pipe Avoider mobile game ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By playing the game and submitting a score, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

Pipe Avoider collects only the minimum needed to run a global leaderboard:

  • Display name — the name you optionally type when submitting a score. This is entirely your choice; you can tap "Skip" and no name is stored. Please do not enter real personal information as your name, as it is shown publicly on the leaderboard.
  • Score and timestamp — the score you achieved and the time it was submitted.
  • Anonymous account identifier — when you submit a score, the app signs in with Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which creates a random identifier. It is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any real-world identity, and you never create an account or log in.
  • Basic usage data — through Google Firebase Analytics, the app collects aggregated, pseudonymous information such as app opens, in-app events, and general device type, used only to understand overall usage and improve the game. Advertising identifiers and ad-personalization are switched off: the app does not collect your device Advertising ID, does not track you across other apps or services, and contains no advertising.
  • Approximate location — analytics records an approximate country and region, derived from your IP address (not from GPS or any device location sensor). We use this only to understand where players are and to help decide which additional languages to support in future. The app does not request location permissions and does not collect your precise or device-level location.

The app does not collect your email address, contacts, precise location, photos, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How we use the information

Information is used solely to operate and maintain the global leaderboard, to keep the app working correctly, and to understand aggregate usage so the game can be improved. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. Legal basis for processing (EEA / UK users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases under the GDPR / UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate interests — to operate the leaderboard you choose to take part in, and to keep the app functioning and secure. Submitting a score and a name is entirely voluntary, and you can use the app without doing so.
  • Consent — where consent is the appropriate basis, you provide it by choosing to submit a score. You can withdraw participation at any time by not submitting scores and by requesting deletion (see below).

For the purposes of the GDPR, the developer of Pipe Avoider is the data controller, and Google LLC acts as a data processor on our behalf.

4. Where the data is stored and international transfers

Leaderboard data and analytics are processed and stored by Google Firebase (Google LLC) on our behalf (the leaderboard database is hosted in the Asia-Southeast region). Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. Submitted display names and scores are publicly visible to other players within the leaderboard.

Because Google operates globally, your information may be processed on servers located outside your own country, including in the United States. Where data is transferred out of the EEA or UK, Google provides appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) under its data processing terms.

5. Content moderation

Because display names are shown publicly, the app filters submitted names against a list of offensive terms and rejects names that match. Players can also report an inappropriate name from within the app, and we reserve the right to remove any leaderboard entry that is offensive, abusive, or otherwise inappropriate.

6. Data retention and deletion

Leaderboard entries are retained while the leaderboard is in operation so it remains meaningful over time. Aggregated analytics are retained according to Google's default retention settings. You can request deletion of a leaderboard entry associated with you at any time.

To request deletion, email keasummit@gmail.com with the subject line Data deletion — Pipe Avoider and tell us the display name and approximate date/score of the entry. We will remove the matching entry within 30 days. Because the data is anonymous, this name is how we locate the entry to delete.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live — including under the EU / UK GDPR — you may have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • request correction (rectification) of inaccurate data;
  • request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten");
  • restrict or object to our processing of your data;
  • data portability; and
  • lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Because the information we hold is anonymous — a self-chosen display name and a score, not tied to your identity — the practical way to exercise these rights is through the deletion request described in section 6. To make any rights request, email keasummit@gmail.com.

8. Children's privacy

Pipe Avoider is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because score submission is optional and anonymous, and advertising identifiers are disabled, players can enjoy the full game without providing any personal information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes, and continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact the developer at keasummit@gmail.com.
