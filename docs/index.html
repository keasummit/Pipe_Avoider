Privacy Policy — Pipe Avoider

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Pipe Avoider mobile game ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By playing the game and submitting a score, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

Pipe Avoider collects only the minimum needed to run a global leaderboard:

  • Display name — the name you optionally type when submitting a score. This is entirely your choice; you can tap "Skip" and no name is stored. Please do not enter real personal information as your name, as it is shown publicly on the leaderboard.
  • Score and timestamp — the score you achieved and the time it was submitted.
  • Anonymous account identifier — when you submit a score, the app signs in with Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which creates a random identifier. It is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any real-world identity, and you never create an account or log in.
  • Usage and device data — through Google Firebase Analytics, the app collects standard, aggregated information such as app opens, in-app events, general device type, and approximate region. This is used only to understand overall usage and improve the game.

The app does not collect your email address, contacts, location (beyond the coarse region inferred by analytics), photos, or any sensitive personal data, and it contains no third-party advertising.

2. How we use the information

Information is used solely to operate and maintain the global leaderboard, to keep the app working correctly, and to understand aggregate usage so the game can be improved. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Where the data is stored and who can access it

Leaderboard data and analytics are processed and stored by Google Firebase (Google LLC) on our behalf, and may be stored on Google's servers (the leaderboard database is hosted in the Asia-Southeast region). Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. Submitted display names and scores are publicly visible to other players within the leaderboard.

4. Content moderation

Because display names are shown publicly, the app filters submitted names against a list of offensive terms and rejects names that match. We also reserve the right to remove any leaderboard entry that is offensive, abusive, or otherwise inappropriate.

5. Data retention and deletion

Leaderboard entries are retained so the leaderboard remains meaningful over time. You can request deletion of a leaderboard entry associated with you at any time.

To request deletion, email keasummit@gmail.com with the subject line Data deletion — Pipe Avoider and tell us the display name and approximate date/score of the entry. We will remove the matching entry within 30 days. Because the data is anonymous, this name is how we locate the entry to delete.

6. Children's privacy

Pipe Avoider is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because score submission is optional and anonymous, players can enjoy the full game without providing any personal information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes, and continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact the developer at keasummit@gmail.com.
